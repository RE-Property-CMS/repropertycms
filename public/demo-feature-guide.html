Feature Guide

Demo Access System

How prospects experience your platform — two ways to get started
Your platform offers two ways to give someone a live, private demo of the full system. Each method creates a completely isolated sandbox with real data pre-loaded — the visitor can explore every feature as Admin, Agent, or Buyer without any setup on their end.
🌐
Scenario 1

Self-Service Demo

A prospect visits your public website, fills in a quick form, and instantly receives their own private demo environment by email — no action needed from you.

How it works
1
Prospect visits the Demo page on your website and enters their name and work email address.
2
The system instantly creates a private sandbox — a unique Admin account, Agent account, and two pre-loaded properties just for them.
3
An email lands in their inbox with their login credentials and direct access links for both the Admin and Agent portals.
4
They click the link, log in with their credentials, and explore freely. When the session ends, all data is automatically deleted.
⏱ 60-minute session 🔒 Fully isolated sandbox 🗑 Auto-deleted on expiry 📧 Work email required
✉️
Scenario 2

Owner-Invited Demo

You personally invite a specific prospect or organisation by entering their email in your admin panel. They receive the same private demo experience — but with a generous 10-day window to explore at their own pace.

How it works
1
You log in to your Admin Panel and go to Demo Sessions → Invite Someone.
2
Enter the prospect's name and email address, then click Send Demo Invitation.
3
The system creates their private sandbox and emails them their credentials — identical to the self-service flow.
4
The prospect has 10 full days to explore. You can monitor their session, resend their credentials if needed, or revoke access early from the admin panel.
📅 10-day session 🔒 Fully isolated sandbox ✉️ Credentials can be resent 🚫 Revocable at any time

📊 What you can do from the Admin Panel

  • View every demo session — both self-service sign-ups and your sent invitations — in one place.
  • Filter sessions by status (Active / Expired) or by type (Self-Service / Invited).
  • Search by email to quickly find a specific prospect's session.
  • Resend credentials to any active session if the prospect didn't receive their email.
  • Revoke any active session immediately — with a confirmation step to prevent accidents.
  • Use the collected emails for follow-up marketing campaigns.
